Black Women

Breaking into Tech

There's a new era of breaking into Tech; Black women are making there presents known in the tech world. The rise of Black women in tech has sparked a high interest leading a great change in the sector. Black women represent only 1.7% of the tech industry's labor force. To address this underrepresentation there are many great resources and mentorships that has come forth for women of color to utilize, learn, and gain knowledge to get their start.

Its so refreshing to see the elevated climb of black women entering into a space they were told they wouldn't be able to understand or gain the knowledge in. Hats off ladies, we love to see the revolution of you all breaking barriers and getting a chance to show us what you are made of; get more insight at black women in tech.
